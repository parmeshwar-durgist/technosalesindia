Yes, our engineering team specializes in custom tooling consulting. You can send us your dimensional blueprint configurations in .dwg or .pdf formats. We will build custom progressive press moulds or grind customized solid carbide drill heads matching your exact dimensional parameters.

Standard stock items (inserts, HSS drills, wrenches) are dispatched within 24 hours and delivered within 2-5 business days depending on delivery cities. Custom gears, spline shafts, and progressive stamping parts require 15-30 days for design alignment, testing, and production runs.

Yes, all heavy geared lathe, drilling, milling, and bandsaw machines distributed by Techno Sales India come with a 12-month structural warranty. Our team handles complete rigging, alignment level checks, dro retrofits and provides on-site diagnostic support during operational failures.
